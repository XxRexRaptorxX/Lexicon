# Basalt Generator

*Lava* will turn to *Basalt* when it is above a *Soul Soil* block while also adjacent to a *Blue Ice* block. 

The *Soul Soil* block **MUST** be beneath the *Lava*, but the *Blue Ice* block can be on any side or above.
