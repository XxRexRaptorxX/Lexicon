# Wind Charge

<ItemImage id="minecraft:wind_charge" />

A <ItemLink id="minecraft:wind_charge" /> is a consumable ranged weapon that can knock entities back and interact with certain [Redstone components](../redstone/redstone_components.md). 

It is used by [*Breezes*](../creatures/monster-breeze.md) as their main attack and can also be shot by [*Players*](../creatures/human-player.md) and 
[<ItemLink id="minecraft:dispenser" />](../redstone/redstone_components.md#dispenser).
