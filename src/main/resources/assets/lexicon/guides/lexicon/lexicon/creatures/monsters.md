# Monsters

In this category, you will find all [creatures](../creatures.md) that are classified as monsters.
This is particularly important for advancements, [enchantments](../enchanting/enchantments.md), [effects](../brewing/effects.md), and other mechanics.

----

### All monsters share the following properties:

- They despawn or don't spawn naturally in Peaceful difficulty
- Prevent the [Player](human-player.md) from sleeping
- Drop more than 1-3 experience orbs
- [Iron Golems](golem-iron_golem.md) and [Snow Golems](golem-snow_golem.md) will target them
- [Parrots](animal-parrot.md) will imitate their sounds
- An active conduit will damage them.
- [Rabbits](animal-rabbit.md) and worried [Pandas](animal-panda.md) will avoid them
- They canâ€™t be leashed
