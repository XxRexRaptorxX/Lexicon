# Arthropods 

In this category, you will find all invertebrate, bug-like [creatures](../creatures.md) in the game.
This is particularly important for advancements, [enchantments](../enchanting/enchantments.md), [effects](../brewing/effects.md), and other mechanics.

----

### All arthropods share the following properties:

- they take extra damage and receive [Slowness IV](../brewing/effects.md#slowness) when hit with a weapon enchanted with [Bane of Arthropods](../enchanting/enchantments.md#bane-of-arthropods)
