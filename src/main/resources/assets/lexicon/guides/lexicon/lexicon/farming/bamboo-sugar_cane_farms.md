# Bamboo/SugarCane Farm

This farm can harvest <ItemLink id="minecraft:bamboo" /> and <ItemLink id="minecraft:sugar_cane" /> fully automatically. 

When a [<ItemLink id="minecraft:observer" />](../redstone/redstone_components.md#observer) realize that something has grown in front of him, 
the [<ItemLink id="minecraft:piston" />](../redstone/redstone_components.md#piston) below destroy the plant and *Water* 
transports the drops to a [<ItemLink id="minecraft:hopper" />](../redstone/redstone_components.md#hopper).

![](auto_bamboo_farm_1.png) ![](auto_bamboo_farm_2.png) ![](auto_bamboo_farm_3.png)

## Bigger Auto Bamboo Farm

Here, instead of *Water*, a <ItemLink id="minecraft:hopper_minecart" /> is used to collect the items, it is more efficient and usually makes more sense on larger farms.
