# Illagers

In this category, you will find all [creatures](../creatures.md) that are classified as illagers. They are the evil counterpart to the [Villagers](human-villager.md).
This is particularly important for advancements, [enchantments](../enchanting/enchantments.md), [effects](../brewing/effects.md), and other mechanics.

----

### All illagers share the following properties:

- The mob attacks [Villagers](human-villager.md) but ignores baby villagers, unlike [Zombie](undead-zombie.md)
- The mob flees from the [Creaking](monster-creaking.md)
- [Witches](human-witch.md) heal the mob in raids
- The mob can join patrols
- The mob can join raids
- They can ride [Ravagers](monster-ravager.md)
