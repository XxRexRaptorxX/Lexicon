# Animal - Camel

__Health:__ 32     
__Attack Damage:__ 0    
__Behavior:__ Passive     
__Spawn:__ Desert Village      
__Breeding:__ Cactus     
__Properties:__       
Can be equipped with a *Saddle*, and they can be ridden in water up to 3 blocks deep with up to two [*Players*](./human-player.md). *Camels* are tall enough that most melee hostile cannot reach the players to attack 
them. They have the ability of 'dashing'. When dashing, the *Camel* launches up to 12 blocks forward and one block upward.
