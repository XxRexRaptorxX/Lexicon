# Waterflow Food Farms

That's still a simple __semi-auto__ farm for crops that can be destroyed by *Water* (like <ItemLink id="minecraft:wheat" />,
<ItemLink id="minecraft:potato" />, <ItemLink id="minecraft:carrot" />,...). 

(The glass is to illustrate the construction) 

- Flipping the [<ItemLink id="minecraft:lever" />](../redstone/redstone_components.md#lever) causes the [*Pistons*](../redstone/redstone_components.md#piston) to lower, allowing *Water* to flow down over the fields and 
  flushing the drops into a [<ItemLink id="minecraft:hopper" />](../redstone/redstone_components.md#hopper) to a [<ItemLink id="minecraft:chest" />](../useables/chest.md).
