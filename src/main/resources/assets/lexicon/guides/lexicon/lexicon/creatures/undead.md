# Undead

In this category, you will find all [creatures](../creatures.md) that are classified as undead.
This is particularly important for advancements, [enchantments](../enchanting/enchantments.md), [effects](../brewing/effects.md), and other mechanics.

----

### All undead mobs share the following properties:

- They are damaged by [Instant Health](../brewing/effects.md#instant-health) and healed by [Instant Damage](../brewing/effects.md#instant-damage)
- They are not affected by [Regeneration](../brewing/effects.md#regeneration) and [Poison](../brewing/effects.md#poison)
- They take extra damage from [Smite](../enchanting/enchantments.md#smite)
- They do not take drowning damage
- They are not targeted by and cannot harm the [Wither](boss-wither.md)
- The majority of undead mobs catch fire under sunlight
