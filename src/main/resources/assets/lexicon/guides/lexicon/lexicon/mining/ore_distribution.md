# Ore Distribution

*Ores* generate at different heights with different probability. 

Some are even less likely to generate in caves (Air exposure).

-----

## Best Ore Layers

[*The Overworld*](../world/dimensions.md#overworld) 
- __<ItemLink id="minecraft:coal_ore" />__: Layer 95 & 136 
- __<ItemLink id="minecraft:copper_ore" />__: Layer 48 
- __<ItemLink id="minecraft:lapis_ore" />__: Layer -1 
- __<ItemLink id="minecraft:iron_ore" />__: Layer 15 & 232 
- __<ItemLink id="minecraft:gold_ore" />__: Layer -16 
- __<ItemLink id="minecraft:redstone_ore" />__: Layer -59 
- __<ItemLink id="minecraft:diamond_ore" />__: Layer -59 
- __<ItemLink id="minecraft:emerald_ore" />__: Layer 236

The greatest variation in ores is around layer 0
