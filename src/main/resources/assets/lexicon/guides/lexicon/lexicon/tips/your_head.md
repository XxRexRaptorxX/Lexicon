# Get your own Head

You can get your own [*Head*](../rare/mob_head.md) with your [*Player*](../creatures/human-player.md) skin in game with a simple [*Command*](../commands.md): 

/give @p minecraft:player_head{SkullOwner:*PLAYER_NAME*}
