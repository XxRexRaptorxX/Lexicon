# Enchanting

[Enchanting](enchanting/enchanting.md) is a mechanic that augments armor, tools, weapons and books with one or more of a variety of [*Enchantments*](enchanting/enchantments.md) 
that improve an item's existing abilities or imbue them with additional abilities and uses.

A special glint animation appears on items that are enchanted.
